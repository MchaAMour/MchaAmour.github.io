Publications of SGO's staff members

Recent publications of the staff can be found from the publication service of the University of Oulu.

Information about publications of SGO's staff members are collected in local database. Lists of publications can be made by different methods:

  • Search by Year gives a collected list of all publications for requested year(s), sorted by the type of publication.
  • Search finds publicatons from the database according to given search instructions.
  • SGO Series gives list of publications in observatory's own publication series.
